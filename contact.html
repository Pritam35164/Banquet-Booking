Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a banquet hall?

Simply search for venues in your preferred city, select your date and guest count, browse available venues, and click "Submit Booking Request" on your chosen venue. The venue owner will review and confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking from all major Indian banks, and UPI payments through Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

You can request booking modifications or cancellations through your dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by venue, so please check the specific venue's terms or contact support for assistance.

How do I list my venue on BanquetBook?

Sign up as a Venue Owner, complete your profile with venue details, photos, and pricing. Our team will review and activate your listing within 24-48 hours.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. Your payment information is never stored on our servers and is processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment processors.

What if I need help during the booking process?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, or live chat. You can reach us at +91 1800-123-4567 or support@banquetbook.in anytime you need assistance.
